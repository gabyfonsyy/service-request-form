Important information — data migration

System access

During the migration process, all users will not have any access to the system during the confirmed downtime schedule.

Data inclusion parameters for migration

The migration includes all historical data of active and inactive employees (e.g. processed payroll, employee profile data, payslips, etc.) before the activity started. Any data being processed while the migration is taking place will not be included.

What's next after submitting this form?

  1. Your CSM will reach out to you for the Work Order form. Please note that data migration activity will NOT commence without a signed Work Order Form. The Work Order will be sent to you within five (5) business days from receipt of email request.
  2. Prior to the Data Migration activity, you will need to fill out an Account Creation Form and Back-End Policy Questionnaire to set up the New Sprout HR URL.
  3. Your CSM will create a new Sprout Payroll account, and your authorized Payroll Admin will need to fill out the Company Profile in Sprout Payroll. Click here as it will direct you to a Knowledge Base site on how to fill out Company Profile.
